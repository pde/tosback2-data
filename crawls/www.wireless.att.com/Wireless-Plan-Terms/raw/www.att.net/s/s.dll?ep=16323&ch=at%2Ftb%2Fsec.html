AT&T Internet Security Suite

AT&T Internet Security Suite powered by McAfee®
Using the Internet means being smart about how you surf and interact online to avoid falling prey to viruses, spyware, and other online threats. The good news is AT&T High Speed Internet members can protect themselves and their PC's with the AT&T Internet Security Suite – powered by McAfee®. This suite is FREE* to qualifying members and includes anti-virus, anti-spyware and firewall protection so you can surf, search and transact online with confidence.

The McAfee® Advantage
The AT&T Internet Security Suite includes revolutionary McAfee® Active Protection technology, which provides the fastest protection against malicious threats to your PC. New and emerging threats are analyzed and blocked in milliseconds, so you don't have to wait for regular updates to arrive.

Download and install the AT&T Internet Security Suite now by clicking on the download button below!

*available at no charge to AT&T U-verse High Speed Internet and AT&T High Speed Internet customers who have a service with a maximum speed of 3.0 or higher. For AT&T High Speed Internet customers who have a service with a maximum downstream speed of 1.5 Mbps or lower, the AT&T Internet Security Suite is an optional service for only $5 per month.

What does AT&T Internet Security Suite - powered by McAfee® do?

Stops Viruses – Detects, blocks and removes viruses before they get to your PC

Stops Hackers – Protects and conceals your computer from hackers with two-way firewall protection

Blocks Spyware – Detects, blocks and removes spyware and adware before it installs on your computer, and removes existing spyware.

Safe Search, Safe Surf – McAfee® SiteAdvisor adds security ratings to Web sites to help you avoid online dangers and alerts you to web sites that may try to steal your identity

Security-as-a-Service – It automatically updates your computer with the latest software and threat data so you're always up-to-date.

Faster Performance – Recent enhancements helped increase performance so it's faster and requires less of your computer's resources than previous versions.

Intelligent Protection – Defers tasks, updates, and alerts when you're viewing movies or gaming in full-screen mode to provide protection without interference.

QuickClean – Safely removes junk files that slow down your PC

Shredder – Allows you to effectively remove any and all traces of confidential files from your computer

Disk Defragmenter – Consolidates fragmented files and folders for better PC health
