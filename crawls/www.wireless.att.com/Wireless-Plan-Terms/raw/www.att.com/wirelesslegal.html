Wireless Legal Documents Home Page

Welcome to AT&T and to the Wireless Legal Documents Home Page. We're pleased that
you've selected us as your wireless carrier. This site is provided to you as a convenience
so that you can go to one place to review the documents and policies that govern your use
of AT&T's wireless services. You can print any of the documents included on this site.

 

Wireless Customer Agreement

The primary document that explains the relationship between you and AT&T when you subscribe to postpaid wireless service from AT&T. If you are an enterprise customer (business or government), click here for additional terms that govern your use of AT&T's wireless service.

 

GoPhone® Customer Agreement and Plans

Plan terms and the agreement between you and AT&T when you subscribe to prepaid wireless service from AT&T.

 

DataConnect Pass/iPad™/Session-Based Wireless Data Services Agreement

Explanation of the relationship between you and AT&T when you subscribe to one of AT&T's prepaid wireless data plans.

 

Applications, Services, and Features

Wireless Application Terms includes terms and conditions governing the relationship between you and AT&T for applications you can download to or use in connection with your AT&T wireless device. Wireless Feature/Service Terms includes terms and conditions governing your relationship with AT&T in connection with features and services related to your wireless voice or data services from AT&T.

 

General Wireless Policies and Processes

Miscellaneous statements or disclosures concerning AT&T policies and practices governing use of AT&T websites and/or the purchase of or subscription to AT&T wireless products and services from AT&T.

 

Other Charges Applicable to Wireless Services

Additional charges that may be applicable to use of wireless services from AT&T not included within the one-time download charge for applications or in the monthly rates applicable to wireless service plans or features.
