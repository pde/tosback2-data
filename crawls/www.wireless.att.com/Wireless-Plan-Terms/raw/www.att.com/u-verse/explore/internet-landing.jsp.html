Stay connected at home and on the go

  • Built-in wireless home networking capability included. Our Residential Gateway is powerful enough to virtually eliminate wireless dead spots and safeguard against outside access of your Internet connection. Also includes four Ethernet ports for wired LAN connections.
  • AT&T Wi-Fi—email, watch streaming video, listen to music, and much more—all on the go. Includes access at home and on-the-go to the entire national AT&T Wi-Fi Hot Spot network, at no extra charge.
Better Communication
  • AT&T Mail Plus at no extra cost with virtually unlimited storage, 10 additional email accounts, and POP access.
  • AT&T Messenger with high-quality video and Enhanced Voice Communication that allows new voice-centric features such as call logs and voice mail
More Convenience
  • Personalized AT&T home page and customized browser
  • Photo storage with Flickr™
  • Unlimited nationwide dial-up Internet access for away from home use
  • Check out AT&T ConnecTechSM for more extensive computer/home network installation and maintenance options.
